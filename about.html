About Crescent Developments

Building premium digital solutions for local businesses

Our Mission

At Crescent Developments, we believe every business deserves access to premium digital solutions. We're dedicated to empowering local businesses in the Tri-Cities area with custom mobile and web applications that drive growth and enhance customer experiences.

We don't believe in cookie-cutter solutions. Every business is unique, and your app should be too. We take the time to understand your specific needs, challenges, and goals to deliver a solution that truly fits your business.

Why Choose Us

Fast Delivery

We deliver production-ready apps in less than 30 days. Your business can't wait months for digital solutions, and neither should you.

Premium Quality

We use modern technologies and best practices to build apps that are fast, reliable, and beautiful. Quality is never compromised.

Custom Solutions

No templates, no shortcuts. Every app is built from scratch to match your exact requirements and brand identity.

Local Partnership

Based in Pasco, Washington, we understand the Tri-Cities market. We're invested in your success and the growth of our local community.

Ongoing Support

We don't disappear after launch. We provide continued support, updates, and improvements to keep your app performing at its best.

Transparent Process

You'll be involved every step of the way with regular updates, demos, and clear communication throughout the development process.

Meet the Founder

Cooper Cazier

Founder & Lead Developer

At 18 years old, Cooper brings several years of coding expertise and a passion for building innovative software solutions. Specializing in Swift and React Native, Cooper founded Crescent Developments with a mission to make premium mobile and web applications accessible to local businesses in the Tri-Cities area.

With a track record of delivering production-ready apps in record time, Cooper combines technical excellence with a deep understanding of business needs. Every project is approached with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to exceeding client expectations.

Cooper's journey into software development began with a curiosity for how apps work and evolved into a passion for creating solutions that solve real business problems. From AI-powered applications to custom booking systems, Cooper has demonstrated the ability to turn complex ideas into elegant, user-friendly software.

Technical Expertise

  • Swift for native iOS development
  • React Native for cross-platform mobile apps
  • Modern web technologies and Progressive Web Apps
  • API integration and backend development
  • UI/UX design and prototyping
